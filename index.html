Whatcom Deserves a Public Hospital

We are worth it. Whatcom county residents deserve a hospital that works more closely in tune with the people. It won't solve every problem immediately yet it is worth doing. It may be a long term project but we can build momentum.

Rome wasn't built in a day. If you plan for Whatcom's future, plan for a public hospital.

Better Off Public: Quality of Care

Peacehealth comes with costs. Peacehealth has made cuts to services and a made push for providers to see more patients1. A public hospital could be more responsive and funded with increased tax dollars as Peacehealth currently benefits from reduced taxes.

Better Off Public: Freedom of Religion in Healthcare

Promote freedom of expression and protections for more diversity within healthcare by providing a public hospital. This includes for those of different faiths and secular people alike than just currently promoted by PeaceHealth: public institutions provide more protection.
